MK2 RS 1800

The Ford RS1800 launch date was 10th June 1975 with a price of £2825 for Basic model and £2990 for the custom version, with a plusher interior. By the end of production in September 1977, this had risen to £4274.

Information

Three cars were built for the 1975 Geneva Motor Show: a motorsport car, a white road car, and a red car, all left-hand drive. All later cars were produced in white apart from a further two known red cars.

Engine

The RS 1800 is Powered by a double overhead camshaft, 1.8 litre BDA naturally aspirated 4 cylinder engine, with 4 valves per cylinder that produces power and torque figures of 113.5 bhp (115 PS/85 kW) at 6000 rpm and 170.6 N·m (126 lb·ft/17.4 kgm) at 3750 rpm respectively. The engine transmits power to the wheels through a 4 speed manual transmission. The Ford Escort RS1800 weighs a claimed 914 kg at the kerb. The Ford Escort RS1800 is claimed to manage a maxiumum speed of 185 km/h (115 mph).
