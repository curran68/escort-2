MK2 RS 2000

This is the Mk2 Escort RS2000, A car that is over 40 years old and commands serious money. First appearing in 1976, even today the Mk2 Escort RS2000 ticks all the boxes for a fun and affordable performance car.

Information

The Mk2 Escort RS2000 ticks all the boxes… Older performance cars, classics if you want to call them that, are usually treated as museum pieces to look at and admire. There are exceptions to this rule in all cars, but the number one is the Mk2 Escort RS2000, Rear-wheel drive, 950kg, cool styling inside and out, and eye watering paintwork options that makes the current Focus RS and ST colours look tame; it’s clear why the RS2K was the number one car to tune in the late 70s and 80s.
