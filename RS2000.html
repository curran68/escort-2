MK2 RS 2000

This is the Mk2 Escort RS2000, A car that is over 40 years old and commands serious money. First appearing in 1976, even today the Mk2 Escort RS2000 ticks all the boxes for a fun and affordable performance car.

Information

Tuneable, fun and seriously cool, the Mk2 Escort RS2000 ticks all the boxes… Older performance cars, classics if you want to call them that, are usually treated as museum pieces to look at and admire, but are rarely used hard. In some respects we understand that as they are very valuable and rare, fixing them or replacing a destroyed one comes at huge expense, but for us this always seems a waste. There are exceptions to this rule in all cars, but the number one is the Mk2 Escort RS2000, but is far more commonly seen squatting under hard acceleration or sideways in a cloud of tyre smoke than it is being polished up for display. Two-litre engine, rear-wheel drive, 950kg, cool styling inside and out, and eye watering paintwork options that makes the current Focus RS and ST colours look tame; it’s clear why the RS2K was the number one car to tune in the late 70s and 80s.

Engine

SPEC FORD ESCORT RS2000 MK2 (1976-1980) Engine 2-litre 8 valve (Pinto Engine) Which had an overhead camshaft engine. Power 110bhp Torque 130lb ft 0-60mph 8.9seconds Top speed 110mph
